How It Works

Two simple phases.

Before you lift, you pay less. After you lift, you pay a fixed amount.

Phase 1 — Before Your Lift

Each month you pay the "Monthly Chit" amount — a discounted rate that decreases over time. The earlier the month, the higher the chit payment.

Phase 2 — After Your Lift

Once you receive your payout, you pay the fixed "Post Lift" amount of $2,350/month for all remaining months until the chit ends.
