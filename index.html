Mechanics

How payment works.

Before you lift

Pay the Monthly Chit amount — a discounted rate that rises the longer you wait to lift.

After you lift

Pay the flat Post Lift amount of $2,350 for every remaining month.

Total Paid = Σ (Chit payments up to lift month) + ($2,350 × remaining months)
