Terms & Conditions — 15-Month Chit Fund

By registering, you confirm that you understand and agree to the following:

1. Participation Commitment. You commit to making monthly contributions as scheduled for the full 15-month duration of the fund. Missing payments may result in forfeiture of your turn or removal from the group at the organiser's discretion.

2. Lift Month Allocation. Lift months are assigned by lottery. All registered participants will be entered into a fair draw, and results will be announced before the fund commences. No gift card compensation is provided for lift month assignments, as gifts are reserved as rewards for on-time payments.

3. Community Trust. This fund operates on mutual trust among all participants. You agree not to default on payments and to communicate any financial difficulties to the organiser promptly.

4. No Guarantee. The 15-Month Chit Fund is a community savings arrangement, not a regulated financial product. Returns and terms are subject to the agreed group rules and are not guaranteed by any financial institution.

5. Data Use. Your personal information (name, phone, email, address) will be used solely to manage your participation in this fund and will not be shared with third parties without your consent.

6. Right to Withdraw. You may withdraw your registration before the fund officially starts by notifying the organiser. Once the fund commences, withdrawal terms will be negotiated on a case-by-case basis.

7. Governing Rules. All participants are subject to the house rules published on this site. The organiser's decision on any disputes is final and binding.
You must agree to the terms before registering.
